Communication Guidelines

Tone and Style

DO:

  • ‚úÖ Be transparent and honest
  • ‚úÖ Use clear, simple language
  • ‚úÖ Provide specific timelines
  • ‚úÖ Take responsibility for issues
  • ‚úÖ Show appreciation for patience
  • ‚úÖ Provide actionable next steps

DON'T:

  • ‚ùå Use technical jargon (unless audience is technical)
  • ‚ùå Make promises you can't keep
  • ‚ùå Blame others or make excuses
  • ‚ùå Minimize impact or severity
  • ‚ùå Leave stakeholders guessing
  • ‚ùå Forget to follow up
